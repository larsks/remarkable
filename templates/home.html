[Remarkable][] is a tool for synchronizing an HTML slideshow among many participants. While it was originally written to drive slideshows created with [remark][], it will work with any HTML slideshow that uses numeric anchors to address slides (i.e., slides URLs end with `#1`, `#2`, etc. ## Starting a presentation 1. Connect to <a href="/present" target="_parent">the presenter interface</a>. 1. Select an identifier for your presentation and enter it in the *Name:* field. This identifier will be used in the URLs that you send out to your audience. 1. Enter a password in the *Secret:* field to prevent someone else from taking control of the presentation. If you navigate away from the presentation or close your browser you will need this secret to continue the presentation. 1. Enter the URL of your presentation in the *Base URL:* field. This should be the fully qualified URL to your presentation, absent any anchor. 1. Click *Create*. 1. Send participants the URL displayed in the *Watch URL:* field. 1. Use the navigation controls to drive the presentation. ## Controlling a presentation The *Go to Start* button will take your audience to the first slide in your presentation. Use the *+1* and *+5* buttons to move forward in a presentation (and the *-1* and *-5* buttons to move backwards). The *Sync Slide* button will set all participants to whatever slide number is set in the *Current slide* field. The *Sync URL* button will set all participants to whatever URL is entered in the *Current URL* field. Note that this will often not work as you expect, because many sites may force a browser redirect or may simply not work inside an HTML frame. ## Watching a presentation Your presenter will send you a link of the form `http://remarkable.oddbit.com/watch/mypresentation` (where *mypresentation* is some identifier your presenter has selected to identify the show). Connect to this URL with your browser, sit back, and enjoy the show. The actual slide URL will be displayed at the top of each slide; you should use this if you want to bookmark a slide (or the entire presentation). [remarkable]: http://remarkable.oddbit.com/ [remark]: https://github.com/gnab/remark
